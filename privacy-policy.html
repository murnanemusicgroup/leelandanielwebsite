PRIVACY POLICY

Last Revised: June 10, 2025

Welcome to LeelandDaniel.com (the "Site"), the official website for Leeland Daniel. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding any information collected on this Site. By using our Site, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Data Collection

We want to be clear: **LeelandDaniel.com does not collect any personal data or sensitive information from visitors.** We do not have user accounts, contact forms that store personal data, or any other features designed to collect identifiable information directly on this Site.

2. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use a limited number of **essential cookies** on LeelandDaniel.com. These cookies are solely for:

  • **Remembering your preferences:** For example, remembering your cookie consent choices so you don't have to confirm them repeatedly.
  • **Anonymous site usage:** Basic, non-personal data (like pages visited, time spent on site) to understand how the site is being used, which helps us improve your Browse experience. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.

We utilize a cookie consent banner to provide transparency and control over these essential cookies. Your interaction with the banner helps us ensure we are only using cookies you've agreed to.

3. External Merchandise Store

Please note that while merchandise is featured on LeelandDaniel.com, all sales and transactions are handled on our external e-commerce website, **store.leelandaniel.com**. Any personal data you provide, such as payment information or shipping addresses, when making a purchase on store.leelandaniel.com is collected and processed by that external platform and its partners (such as Spreadshirt and payment processors like PayPal, Visa, Mastercard, American Express). Their data collection, usage, and privacy practices are governed by their own respective privacy policies, which you should review before making a purchase. LeelandDaniel.com does not receive or store any personal data from these transactions.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

LeelandDaniel.com is a general audience website and is **intended for children under 13** as well as older users. We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) in the United States. Since we do not collect any personal information from anyone visiting our Site, including children under 13, there are no specific COPPA-related data collection or parental consent mechanisms required for this Site.

5. User Rights and Data Access

As we do not collect or store any personal data from visitors on LeelandDaniel.com, we do not have any personal data to provide access to, correct, or delete.

6. Data Security

Given that we do not collect or store any personal data on this Site, there is no personal data to protect from breaches or unauthorized access. Our focus is on maintaining the security of the website itself.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Revised" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
