Biography

Short Bio: Leeland Daniel is an emerging artist known for his soulful vocals and heartfelt songwriting. With a unique blend of contemporary R&B and pop influences, he crafts music that resonates deeply with listeners.

Medium Bio: Born in Oklahoma City, OK and raised in Carmel, IN, Leeland Daniel discovered his passion for music at a young age. Drawing inspiration from Pop music, and R&B, he developed a distinctive sound characterized by layers of harmonies and simple instrumentals. His recent single, "out of love," has garnered 100 streams in the first week, setting the stage for a promising career.

Long Bio: Leeland Daniel (b. Leeland Daniel Murnane, on December 31, 2009) is a singer/songwriter, and producer from Indianapolis, Indiana. He is currently signed to his own label, LmDaniel Music which is licensed by Murnane Records, a division of Murnane Music Group.

He is the co-founder of Murnane Music Group, which was founded by Marc Murnane.

Since starting at just 12 years old, Leeland has released 2 solo singles under the name Leeland Daniel. His stage name, GoldenCraftr was the very beginning of Leeland's music career. He released his first song, Love You, on November 11, 2022, which was co-written by fellow producer, writer, and artist, Stephen Flickner He then went on and released his first EP, "Messing with my Feelings" which consisted of three tracks, Love You, Can't Love You, and Without You. GoldenCraftr has since been retired.

Leeland Daniel's first single, "falling" was originally released under his stage name "GoldenCraftr" on February 7th, 2025 But shortly after he changed the artist name.

In February 2025, Leeland rebranded from "GoldenCraftr" to "Leeland Daniel" he left his stage name in the past because he wanted a fresh start with his music.

Leeland stated on his public Snapchat story, "I am no longer GoldenCraftr. I decided to make a fresh start with my music and in the next week or so, my artist profiles will be found under the name Leeland Daniel. The only song that is transferring artist profiles, is my newest one. (falling)".
